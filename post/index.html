Introduction Besides altering your algorithms, another easy way to improve the performance of your code is by reducing heap allocations. Compared to the heap, stack allocations are significantly faster to both allocate and free, as they are usually done by simply updating a pointer, potentially in a single operation, and often by a dedicated CPU register. The heap on the other hand is a complicated beast.
